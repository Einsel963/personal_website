These notes will review equilibrium statistical mechanics and selected topics
in non-equilibrium physics, including ensembles, partition functions,
fluctuations, response theory, and entropy.

The long-term aim is to connect statistical-mechanical ideas with open quantum
systems and many-body dynamics.
