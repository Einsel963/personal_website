These notes collect my ongoing study of group theory and Lie algebras for
physics. The focus is on continuous symmetries, matrix Lie groups, tangent
spaces near the identity element, generators, commutators, and the geometric
meaning of infinitesimal transformations.

The long-term goal is to connect group-theoretic language with quantum
mechanics, angular momentum theory, representation theory, and field-theoretic
applications.
