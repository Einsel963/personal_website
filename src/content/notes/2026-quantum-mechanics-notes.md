These notes will summarize selected topics in quantum mechanics, including
Hilbert spaces, operators, angular momentum, perturbation theory, identical
particles, and the conceptual structure of measurement.

They are intended as a bridge between undergraduate quantum mechanics and more
advanced topics in quantum information and many-body theory.
