These notes will collect mathematical tools frequently used in theoretical
physics, including complex analysis, Fourier methods, Green's functions,
special functions, tensor notation, and basic differential geometry.

The goal is to organize the technical background needed for field theory,
electrodynamics, and quantum theory.
