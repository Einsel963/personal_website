This planned talk will introduce the basic structure of multipartite Bell
scenarios, with emphasis on the $(3,2,2)$ setting, Mermin and Svetlichny-type
inequalities, correlation tensors, and the distinction between fully
separable, biseparable, and genuinely multipartite entangled resources.
